---
title: "AA91: Beneficiary Transfer Failure"
description: "Troubleshooting issues with EntryPoint's fund transfer to beneficiary"
---

The EntryPoint encountered an error when attempting to transfer accrued funds from the user operation to the designated beneficiary.

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. For Self-Managed Bundlers:
   - Verify your bundler configuration
   - Ensure the beneficiary address is valid and can receive transfers

2. For Bundler Service Users:
   - If you're using a provider like JiffyScan, [reach out to their support](https://t.me/JiffyScan)

Addressing these configuration issues should resolve the AA91 error and enable successful fund transfers.
