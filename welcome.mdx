## What is JiffyLabs
JiffyLabs is on of the leading Infra service provider for teams builiding on Account Abstraction and Chain Abstraction. Our products include our leading AA Explorer i.e `Jiffyscan`, `Paymaster`, `Bundler` and `Unified Data APIs`.

## What is JiffyScan
JiffyScan is leading 4337 UserOp Explorers for EVM chains. The views are designed keeping the abstract entities in mind, not the underlying node and blockchain working. It is the tool used across the industry to verify the submission and acceptance of a userOp.
